Fast 2D LPs

Fast 2D LPs

Since vectors in the plane can be ordered by the angle with respect to the positive real axis, we can efficiently evaluate the support vector of a polygon in constraint representation by comparing normal directions, provided that its edges are ordered. We use the symbol $\preceq$ to compare directions, where the increasing direction is counter-clockwise.
